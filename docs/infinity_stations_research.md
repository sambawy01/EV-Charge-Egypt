## Research Methodology

### Sources Used
1. **Google Maps Places API** - Primary source. Searched for "Infinity charging station",
   "Infinity-e charging station", "Infinity EV station" across all major Egyptian cities
   and governorate capitals. Also used coordinate-based radius searches.
2. **OpenChargeMap API** - Queried for all EV stations in Egypt (countrycode=EG).
   API returned no results at time of query (may be temporarily down).
3. **PlugShare API** - Requires authentication; public API endpoints did not return data.

### Cities/Regions Searched
Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh, Luxor, Aswan, Ismailia, Suez,
Port Said, Fayoum, Minya, Asyut, Sohag, Beni Suef, Tanta, Mansoura, Zagazig,
Damanhour, Kafr El Sheikh, Damietta, Matruh, El Alamein, Borg El Arab,
New Administrative Capital, 10th of Ramadan City, Obour City, Badr City,
Madinaty, Shorouk City, Rehab City, 6th of October City, Sheikh Zayed City,
Dahab, Safaga, Marsa Alam, El Gouna, Ain Sokhna, Sadat City, Shibin el Kom

### Notes
- Stations are deduplicated by Google Place ID to avoid double-counting
- Names appear as both "Infinity Charging Station" and "Infinity-e Charging Station"
- Some stations may be located at gas stations (ChillOut, Wataneya/National)
- Some stations are at commercial/residential developments (New Giza, SODIC, Palm Hills, Madinaty)
- The 70 stations found represent approximately 35% of the claimed 200+ stations
- Many stations are likely only visible in the Infinity-e mobile app and not listed on Google Maps
- Upper Egypt (Luxor, Aswan, Minya, Asyut, Sohag) stations were not found on Google Maps
  despite Infinity claiming presence there - these may be newer installations
